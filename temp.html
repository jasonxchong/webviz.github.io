Max Temperature

As expected max temperatures have the highest averages for those cities closest to the equator. From the scatter plot we can clearly see the trend as cities further away from the equator have lower max tempatures. While it also appears that more cities in the northern hempisphere may have lower max temps, it's also important to know that there are more cities within the most northern latitude points. Since the sample was selected randomly this should be reflective of the general max temperature averages.
